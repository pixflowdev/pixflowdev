# Neobrutalism Layouts

Use these as composition patterns. Adapt the subject matter to the run brief.

## Loud Offer Hero

Purpose: make the style and offer instantly recognizable.

Structure:

- Compact nav with hard-border logo mark and one raised CTA.
- Small saturated label or badge.
- Oversized blunt headline with one short lead.
- Chunky CTA button and optional secondary action.
- Right-side or lower artifact: book, dashboard, kit, offer card, product slab, checklist, or campaign card.
- Optional decorative squares behind content, never carrying meaning.

Rules:

- Hero must not be a plain centered text block.
- The artifact should have a strong hard shadow and real labels.
- Use one loud accent area, not five competing accents.

## Problem And Proof Cards

Purpose: turn raw style into useful page logic.

Structure:

- Two or three heavy cards.
- Each card has eyebrow, heading, concise body, and optionally a small graphic mark.
- One card may use yellow or violet fill; others stay off-white.

Rules:

- Cards need enough padding after border and shadow.
- Do not use equal emphasis for every card if the copy has a natural priority.

## Checklist Or Lessons Grid

Purpose: show practical value in an easily scannable block.

Structure:

- Section label plus heavy heading.
- Two-column grid on desktop, single-column on mobile.
- Each row/card has a small square mark, short title, and optional support text.

Rules:

- Keep copy short enough to preserve chunky rhythm.
- Do not make tiny icon-only rows. The text is the value.

## Artifact Preview Section

Purpose: provide a concrete object or product proof.

Structure:

- Large shadowed panel or card.
- Preview could be a book cover, dashboard, pricing package, product checklist, prompt kit, or template board.
- Include captions, chapter rows, package items, status chips, or data labels.

Rules:

- The artifact must feel inspectable from a screenshot.
- Avoid empty abstract rectangles.

## Saturated CTA Band

Purpose: close with confidence.

Structure:

- Full-width or wide card with violet or yellow fill.
- Thick black border.
- Large direct heading.
- One CTA and one optional secondary proof line.

Rules:

- Text contrast must pass.
- Do not add gradients or blur effects.
