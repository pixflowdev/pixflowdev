FAQ

Recipe questions

When should I use it?

Use it for creator launches, bold product pages, digital guides, campaign surfaces, or dashboards that should feel raw and graphic.

What should stay consistent?

Keep the black border, hard offset shadow, vivid yellow/violet accents, flat fills, and obvious focus states consistent.

What can go wrong?

Too many loud blocks can collapse into noise. Use hierarchy, not equal emphasis, and leave enough spacing for shadows.

Does it work on mobile?

Yes, but cards must stack cleanly, shadows need room, and long labels must wrap without horizontal overflow.

How should Stage A finish?

Run the checklist: contrast, shadow direction, mobile overflow, real content roles, and no generic filler.
