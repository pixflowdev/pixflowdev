# Clean Checklist

Read this before writing the artifact and again before handoff.

## Required Reads

- `design-method.md`
- `method-skill.md`
- Active Pixflow Design design system first; `method-design-system.md` only as fallback reference.
- `method-layouts.md`
- `method-components.md`
- `method-example.html`

Use optional files when available:

- `method-responsive.md`
- `method-accessibility.md`
- `method-anti-patterns.md`
- `method-critique.md`
- `method-image-manifest.json`

## Pre-Write Checklist

- The route is appropriate for Clean: product, SaaS, app, workflow, plugin, dashboard, or platform.
- The page has a concrete product outcome, not a vague category headline.
- The plan includes early inspectable product proof.
- The plan includes enough commercial support for the route: logos, pricing, testimonials, integrations, security, compatibility, or support.
- The plan avoids external source branding and copied preview layout.
- The plan uses 1600px desktop review width and avoids skinny desktop columns.

## Final Stage A Checklist

- The header is sparse and production-ready, not a cluttered marketing nav.
- The hero uses a large centered H1 with a concrete product outcome.
- All visible typography is modern sans-serif; no serif display headings, editorial mastheads, or magazine styling.
- CSS does not include `body` in a `font: inherit` reset; the body font-family must render as sans-serif in screenshots.
- The first viewport has a clear CTA row and does not feel unfinished.
- Product proof is specific to the brief and appears early.
- Cards/devices contain readable UI labels, statuses, controls, code, rows, or states.
- The page uses one accent color and does not drift into decorative gradients.
- Feature cards are useful, not generic marketing slogans.
- Commercial proof sections are believable and route-appropriate.
- Badges, chips, buttons, and mockup labels stay inside containers.
- Mobile layout preserves centered hero rhythm and avoids horizontal overflow.
- Product claims are believable and not over-specific without source material.
- The visible page does not mention external recipe sources, test harnesses, Bricks conversion, or internal file names.
- Stage A output is standalone HTML/CSS and does not include Bricks JSON.

## Handoff Bar

The artifact is not ready unless it would be useful as a Pixflow iframe preview without explanation.
