# Bricks Service Detail Checklist

## P0

- [ ] The page stays focused on one service.
- [ ] The hero states audience, outcome, and enquiry action.
- [ ] Process and expectations are clear.
- [ ] Objections or FAQs answer real decision concerns.
- [ ] No prices, guarantees, credentials, or testimonials are invented.
- [ ] All generated classes are short BEM names.
- [ ] Pixflow variables are used for colors, borders, radius, spacing, and buttons.

## P1

- [ ] Benefits, inclusions, and process use different layout families.
- [ ] Service-specific media or proof panels are purposeful.

## P2

- [ ] Final CTA feels distinct from the preceding section.
