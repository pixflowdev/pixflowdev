# Bricks Service Detail Page Skill

Use this pack for one focused service page. The page should explain who the service is for, the outcome, what is included, how the process works, common objections, and the next enquiry step.

Keep the page specific to one service. Do not drift into a broad services overview, do not invent prices or credentials, and do not repeat the same benefit wording across multiple sections.
