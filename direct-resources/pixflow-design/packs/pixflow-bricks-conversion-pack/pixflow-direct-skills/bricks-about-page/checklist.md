# Bricks About Page Checklist

## P0

- [ ] The page has a clear point of view, not generic mission copy.
- [ ] Story, values, and approach are distinct sections.
- [ ] No founders, team members, awards, dates, or credentials are invented.
- [ ] At least one proof or trust cue appears before the final CTA.
- [ ] All generated classes are short BEM names.
- [ ] Pixflow variables are used for generated styling.

## P1

- [ ] Values are not all equal cards.
- [ ] Media/proof panels have a clear role.

## P2

- [ ] Final CTA connects naturally to the story.
