# Bricks Contact Page Style Spec Contract

## Token Rules

- Contact panels use `var(--foreground)`.
- Form-like fields and buttons use `var(--radius--btn)` and `var(--border--btn)`.
- Visible field borders use `max(var(--border--btn), 1px)` and `var(--border)`.
- Larger panels use `var(--radius--surface)`.

## Layout Style Rules

- Prioritize clarity and tap targets over decorative complexity.
- Keep reassurance close to the form.
- Make mobile stacking simple and predictable.
