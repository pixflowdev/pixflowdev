# Bricks Contact Page Layout References

## Default Rhythm

1. `contact_intro_split` - primary contact form or details split.
2. `contact_details_panel` - practical expectations and support details.
3. `contact_map_split` - location/map panel only when location context exists.
4. `contact_faq_support` - reassurance near the form.
5. `contact_final_cta` - final contact action.

## Layout Rules

- Keep the page focused on enquiry, booking, or contact.
- Do not invent addresses, phone numbers, emails, opening times, or map locations.
- Put expectation-setting copy close to the form or contact action.
