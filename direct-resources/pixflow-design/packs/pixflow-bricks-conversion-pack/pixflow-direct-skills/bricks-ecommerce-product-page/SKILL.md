# Bricks Ecommerce Product Page Skill

Use this pack for ecommerce product detail, collection, shop, and buy-now pages.

Do not invent prices, shipping terms, review counts, ratings, discounts, inventory, or product claims. When product media is missing, create labelled editable image slots instead of unrelated stock imagery.
