# Bricks Ecommerce Product Layout References

## Default Rhythm

1. `product_detail_hero` - product media and purchase action.
2. `product_benefit_bento` - benefits and use cases.
3. `product_specs_comparison` - specs or variants.
4. `product_review_proof` - supplied proof only.
5. `product_buying_faq` - objections.
6. `product_purchase_cta` - final purchase close.

## Layout Rules

- Put purchase action early.
- Do not fabricate prices or proof.
- Avoid unrelated lifestyle stock images when product visuals are not supplied.
