# Bricks Ecommerce Product Style Spec Contract

## Token Rules

- Product media frames use `var(--radius--surface)` and `var(--border--surface)`.
- Cards use `var(--foreground)` and `var(--border)`.
- Purchase CTAs use `var(--radius--btn)`, `var(--border--btn)`, and accent.

## Layout Style Rules

- Keep product hierarchy practical and inspectable.
- Use accent for purchase action and selected option states.
- Keep mobile cards compact enough for repeated buying decisions.
