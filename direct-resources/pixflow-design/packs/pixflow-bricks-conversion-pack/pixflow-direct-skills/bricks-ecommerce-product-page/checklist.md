# Bricks Ecommerce Product Page Checklist

## P0

- [ ] Product name, benefit, media role, and purchase action are clear.
- [ ] No prices, discounts, review counts, ratings, stock levels, or shipping terms are invented.
- [ ] Product media is supplied, clearly requested, or represented by labelled editable placeholders.
- [ ] Buying objections are answered.
- [ ] All generated classes are short BEM names.
- [ ] Pixflow variables are used for generated styling.

## P1

- [ ] Product details/specs are scannable on mobile.
- [ ] Benefits, details, proof, and CTA use different layout families.

## P2

- [ ] Final purchase CTA is distinct.
