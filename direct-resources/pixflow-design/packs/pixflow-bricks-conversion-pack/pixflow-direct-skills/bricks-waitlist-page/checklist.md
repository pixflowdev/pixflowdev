# Bricks Waitlist Page Checklist

## P0

- [ ] Signup action is visible in the hero and final CTA.
- [ ] The page explains what happens after joining.
- [ ] No launch dates, user counts, partner logos, or beta metrics are invented.
- [ ] The page stays focused and does not become a full SaaS homepage by default.
- [ ] All generated classes are short BEM names.
- [ ] Pixflow variables are used for generated styling.

## P1

- [ ] Product or launch preview is editable and not a stock image.
- [ ] FAQ answers practical signup concerns.

## P2

- [ ] Final signup CTA is visually distinct.
