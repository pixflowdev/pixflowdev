# Bricks Product Spec Style Spec Contract

- Use active Pixflow Framework tokens as final authority: background, foreground, text, muted, accent, border, radius, and spacing.
- Dense information surfaces need clear labels, subdued borders, and readable contrast.
- Accent colour is reserved for status, selected states, and primary action; avoid decorative colour noise.
- Avoid one-note palettes and avoid hiding caveats or assumptions in low-contrast text.
