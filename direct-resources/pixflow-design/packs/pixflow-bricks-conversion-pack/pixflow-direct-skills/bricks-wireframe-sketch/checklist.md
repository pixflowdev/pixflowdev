# Bricks Wireframe Sketch Checklist

## P0

- [ ] Output stays low fidelity.
- [ ] Every region is labelled or has a clear content role.
- [ ] No stock images, gradients, polished mockups, or decorative visual effects are introduced.
- [ ] Section shapes vary enough to communicate rhythm.
- [ ] Final output remains editable Bricks JSON with short BEM classes.
- [ ] Pixflow variables are used for neutral structure styling.

## P1

- [ ] Mobile stacking is explicit and simple.
- [ ] CTA/action zones are visible.

## P2

- [ ] Wireframe can be upgraded later without rebuilding the structure.
