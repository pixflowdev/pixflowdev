# Bricks Local Service Checklist

## P0

- [ ] Local/service context is visible in the hero.
- [ ] Services are differentiated and not repeated in multiple equal card grids.
- [ ] Contact or booking path is practical and clear.
- [ ] No fake addresses, opening hours, reviews, or credentials.
- [ ] Pixflow token object shapes are used for surfaces, forms, buttons, and images.

## P1

- [ ] Proof appears before or directly after the service overview.
- [ ] Process or expectation-setting reduces enquiry anxiety.

## P2

- [ ] Contact section is mobile-first and easy to scan.
