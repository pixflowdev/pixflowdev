# Bricks Product Brand Landing Checklist

## P0

- [ ] Product-brand runs include the full rhythm when the prompt asks for a homepage or redesign: header, product hero, heritage/story, flavour or mood discovery, categories, popular products or product showcase, quality/trust, and final CTA.
- [ ] Uploaded screenshots/media are used as visual references for brand colour, section rhythm, product/packaging treatment, and media roles.
- [ ] The page does not look like a SaaS/plugin, local service, or generic corporate landing page.
- [ ] Product/category sections do not collapse into repeated plain card grids.
- [ ] Explicit dates, heritage facts, product names, and brand claims from the prompt/reference are preserved.
- [ ] No prices, certifications, awards, reviews, founder facts, or claims are invented.
- [ ] All styling uses Pixflow token object shapes and short semantic BEM classes.

## P1

- [ ] The first viewport clearly communicates the product/brand category and uses a strong product or packaging visual role.
- [ ] Product browsing feels tangible through product cards, category cards, media slots, flavour tiles, and trust badges.
- [ ] Bright playful brands use controlled colour contrast and readable type rather than random accent cards.

## P2

- [ ] Product mood/flavour tiles use varied shapes, sizes, or colour blocks instead of identical cards.
- [ ] Heritage media and product media have clear alt/prompt intent.
