# Product Brand Layout References

- `brand_navigation_header`: compact brand row for logo/name, product/category navigation, story, contact, and optional language/CTA. Keep it simple and consumer-brand focused.
- `brand_product_hero`: large colour-field hero with short sensory copy, CTA pair, and product/packaging lineup or supplied screenshot media. Avoid browser chrome, dashboards, and SaaS UI panels.
- `heritage_story_split`: two-column story with origin image/media, founding year badge, story copy, and a few fact cards. Preserve supplied dates.
- `mood_product_discovery`: flavour, mood, or feeling tiles. Use shaped cards, bold colours, product examples, and one exploration CTA.
- `product_category_grid`: rounded product category cards with product-media slots and explore links. Category cards should feel like browsing products, not services.
- `popular_products_showcase`: product strip/grid with packaging media, product names, category labels, and optional carousel dots.
- `quality_trust_panel`: honest trust badges and quality copy using only supplied facts.
- `final_brand_cta`: warm closing band with product or contact action, not a generic newsletter footer.
