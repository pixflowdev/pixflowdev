# Bricks Interactions And Animations

- Interactions are stored under `_interactions`.
- Use interactions for deliberate click, hover, scroll, enter-view, load-more, popup, and form behavior.
- Do not use deprecated entrance animation settings as the default approach.
- Animation names and actions should come from Bricks-supported options.
- Keep motion purposeful and accessible; avoid animating every card.
- If an interaction requires state persistence or storage, record it in the plan before compiling.
