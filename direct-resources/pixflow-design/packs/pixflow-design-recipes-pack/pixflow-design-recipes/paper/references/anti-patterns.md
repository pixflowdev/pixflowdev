# Paper Anti-Patterns

Reject these outputs:

- Dark theme takeover caused by system preference.
- Beige editorial report instead of a product page.
- Fake aged paper, torn edges, stains, tape, paperclips, scrapbook clutter, or heavy noise.
- A small centered card preview instead of a full page.
- Generic dashboard metrics where the brief needs documents or workspace proof.
- Equal card grids repeated for every section.
- Four-column desktop feature grids that make cards too thin.
- Low-contrast grey text on warm paper.
- Emoji icons in product cards.
- Placeholder labels such as Feature One, Lorem Ipsum, Dashboard, or Untitled.
- Blue gradient SaaS styling.
- Copy that explains the recipe instead of selling the product.
- Floating suggestions or cards that overlap copy at mobile sizes.

If any appear, repair before handoff.
