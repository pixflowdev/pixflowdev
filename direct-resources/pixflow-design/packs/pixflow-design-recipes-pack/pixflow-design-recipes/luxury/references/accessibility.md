# Luxury Accessibility Rules

- Text contrast must remain strong on dark backgrounds.
- Secondary text cannot rely on very low opacity.
- All links and buttons need visible focus-visible styles.
- Hit targets must be at least 44px high where interactive.
- Do not put essential text only inside images.
- Decorative images can have empty alt text; meaningful product images need concise alt text.
- Respect reduced motion with no hidden initial state.
- Keep uppercase text short enough to read.
- Avoid thin type weights for paragraphs.
