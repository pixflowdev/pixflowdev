The edit

Fewer objects, held with more intention.

Aurum House is built around private selection rather than catalogue speed. The edit gives every console, vessel, chair, and surface enough space for proportion and material to do the work.

Appointments are prepared for interior teams, galleries, and brand environments that need a composed collection view before final placement decisions.

Material proof

Provenance without theatre.

The collection relies on restrained evidence: weight, surface, finish, service path, and the way each object answers an actual room. No decorative claim needs to shout.

Stone

Honed, veined, grounded.

Obsidian and dark marble references keep the forms architectural, with natural irregularity left visible.

Metal

Champagne as an edge.

Warm metal appears as a support, seam, or material sample, never as a decorative flood.
