# Paper Responsive Rules

## Desktop

- Review at 1600px wide.
- Primary container should be around 1440px with responsive gutters.
- Hero text should not exceed about 880px unless centered.
- Editor shell should be broad enough to show sidebar and document pane.
- Feature cards use three columns only when each card has enough width for its miniature UI.

## Tablet

- Nav hides center links or compresses them into a menu.
- Hero intro can stay centered; editor shell sits below.
- Feature cards become two columns.
- Workflow becomes two columns or stacked rows.
- Use-case wide card remains one card; smaller cards can stack.

## Mobile

- Container gutters around 16px to 20px.
- Hero heading around 40px to 48px, line-height no tighter than 1.05.
- CTA buttons stack or wrap cleanly.
- Editor shell hides sidebar and floating suggestions if overlap risk exists.
- Miniature UI plates simplify to rows and labels.
- Pricing cards stack and highlighted plan remains readable.
- Footer columns stack and legal/social row remains visible.

## Overflow Guardrails

- No horizontal scroll.
- No one-word-per-line headings unless the brief explicitly asks for poster type.
- No pill button wider than its container.
- No card grid track below roughly 300px desktop or 260px tablet.
- No floating product card may overlap text on mobile.
