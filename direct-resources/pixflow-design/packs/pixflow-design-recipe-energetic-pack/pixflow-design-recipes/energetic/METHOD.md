# Energetic Recipe

Use this recipe when the brief calls for a lively, energetic brand, a creator/influencer campaign, a modern beverage, fitness products, energetic tools, or launch pages that need to feel bold, powerful, and full of vitality.

## Intent

Create an active, high-impact landing page built around vibrant colors, thick structural borders, hard offset shadows, expressive typography, and snappy interaction states.

This is a visual method. Follow the run brief, uploaded references, selected design system, craft rules, and active Pixflow skill. Use this recipe to shape container styling, border geometry, shadow treatments, pacing, and hover animations.

## Core Rules

- Use thick 4px borders on all primary containers, buttons, and panels.
- Contrast deep indigo/purple with warm peach and bright orange surfaces to create visual energy.
- Use solid offset shadows (`4px 4px 0px 0px`) instead of soft gradients or blurs.
- Keep typography bold, expressive, and uppercase for display titles (Limelight is highly recommended).
- Monospaced fonts (like JetBrains Mono) must be used for badges, details, and micro-labels.
- Interaction states must be explicit, using snappy scaling transitions on hover.
- Keep structural grids balanced (like 2x2 cards) and make the hero product central.

## Signature Page Rhythm

1. Nav header with a thick-bordered brand wordmark and an action button with solid offset shadows.
2. Centered/asymmetric Hero featuring the main product (e.g. beverage can, gear, mockup) with an uppercase bold headline, product badges, and a call-to-action button.
3. Feature section with a structured 2x2 grid of thick-bordered cards showing core ingredients, features, or product stats.
4. Active breakdown or process section with monospaced details and vibrant background bands.
5. Review/testimonial row or carousel using bold quote bubbles or thick-bordered testimonials.
6. Final prominent CTA section promoting an active choice (e.g. "Shop Now", "Get Active") with a thick border and shadow.
7. Footer with monospaced metadata, clean link columns, and a bold copyright/wordmark block.

## Visual DNA

- Background: `#FFEDD5` (Warm Peach) or `#1D1836` (Deep Indigo) for dark contrast sections.
- Borders: Solid 4px `#EA580B` or `#1D1836`.
- Accent: `#EA580B` (Orange) and `#F59E0B` (Amber).
- Typography: Expressive display font, uppercase headings, JetBrains Mono labels.
- Layout: Balanced grids, solid offset shadows, thick outline containers.
- Radius: Softly rounded (8px to 16px) but kept solid by the 4px border.
- Shadows: Solid offset `4px 4px 0px 0px`.
- Motion: Snappy spring-like scaling (`scale(1.05)`) on interactive elements.

## Copy Direction

Use active, punchy, and motivating copy. Incorporate words like charge, vibe, active, power, raw, vital, press, launch, energy, focus, and source. Avoid passive, corporate, or overly technical jargon.

## Responsive Rules

- At desktop width (1600px viewport), ensure split columns do not squeeze text. If a heading wraps awkwardly, collapse the layout or reduce the font size.
- On mobile, collapse grid structures to single-column blocks, keeping the 4px borders and solid shadows readable.
- Maintain a minimum of 44px for touch targets, keeping the offset shadows visually consistent.
- Respect system preferences for reduced motion by disabling scale transitions when requested.
