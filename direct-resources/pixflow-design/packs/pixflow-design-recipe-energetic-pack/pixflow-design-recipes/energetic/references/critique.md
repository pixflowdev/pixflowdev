# Energetic Final Critique

Score the artifact before handoff.

## Composition

- Pass: bold layout, thick-bordered containers, centered hero product mockup, and solid shadows create a highly recognizable energetic first viewport.
- Repair if: the hero has thin borders, soft shadows, or a generic clean SaaS layout.
- Repair if: a two-column section makes a heading, paragraph, image, or card feel squeezed. Use a centered/full-width intro, wider asymmetric tracks, local type scale adjustment, or a one-column stack.

## Typography

- Pass: bold display titles (uppercase) and monospaced badges/details are readable and structured across breakpoints.
- Repair if: display fonts (like Limelight) are used for body copy, or headings clip.

## Imagery & Badges

- Pass: centered product mockups and brackets/slashes monospaced labels support the offer.
- Repair if: imagery is generic decoration or missing thick borders and offset shadows.

## Style Consistency

- Pass: primary orange and secondary amber accents contrast with deep indigo backgrounds, and all main containers use 4px borders and solid offset shadows.
- Repair if: elements use gradients, soft/blurry drop shadows, pastel colors, or thin/delicate lines.

## Responsive

- Pass: mobile preserves hierarchy, layout grids stack to single columns, and there is no text/horizontal overflow.
- Repair if: interactive elements overflow or become squished, or tap targets are smaller than 44px.
