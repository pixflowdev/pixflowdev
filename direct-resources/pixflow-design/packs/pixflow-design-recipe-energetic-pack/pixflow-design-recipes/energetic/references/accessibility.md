# Energetic Accessibility Rules

- Maintain strong color contrast (WCAG 2.2 AA). Bright orange (`#EA580B`) or amber (`#F59E0B`) text must only be used on dark indigo (`#1D1836`) or white backgrounds, never on light orange/peach backgrounds.
- Body text should use a clean, highly legible font (like Inter) with a normal weight (400-500) rather than a display font like Limelight.
- Interactive elements (buttons, inputs, links) must have clear `focus-visible` states, using thick borders or offset outlines.
- All buttons and links must meet the 44px minimum tap target height and width.
- Avoid low-contrast or low-opacity text inside monospaced badges and detail tags.
- Provide descriptive `alt` tags for all product mockup images.
- Respect reduced motion media queries, disabling scaling and translation transitions.
