# Energetic Anti-Patterns

- Thin, delicate, or subtle borders (borders must be thick 4px solid lines).
- Soft, fuzzy drop shadows or complex gradient overlays.
- Low-contrast text on bright backgrounds (e.g. orange text on a peach background).
- Generic minimalist SaaS grids or layouts with no distinct styling.
- Display fonts (like Limelight) used for long paragraph text blocks.
- Overly technical, corporate, or passive marketing language.
- Cluttered, multi-column layouts that squeeze columns and headlines on desktop.
- Missing active hover/focus indicators on buttons.
- Using random, unrelated neon or pastel colors outside the orange/purple palette.
