# Luxury Anti-Patterns

- Pure black page with generic white SaaS cards.
- Gold everywhere, casino energy, or fake premium shine.
- Low-contrast grey paragraphs on black.
- Huge headline that clips on tablet or mobile.
- Dense product catalog grid.
- Empty moodboard with no offer or action.
- Random serif plus random mono without hierarchy.
- Bright gradients, glassmorphism, or neon cyberpunk drift.
- Fake scarcity copy, discounts, countdowns, or hype language.
- Decorative imagery that does not support the section message.
