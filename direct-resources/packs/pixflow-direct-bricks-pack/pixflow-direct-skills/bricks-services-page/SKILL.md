# Bricks Services Page Skill

Use this pack for services, packages, offers, or what-we-do pages.

Differentiate service paths, explain who each service is for, show proof or objections, describe the process, and finish with an enquiry path. Do not list services twice in different words, and do not generate several visually identical card-grid sections.
