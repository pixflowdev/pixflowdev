# Bricks Conditions

- Conditions are show/hide logic stored on the element, not freeform CSS.
- Use conditions only when the prompt needs role, login, post, date, viewport, query, or context-specific visibility.
- Do not hide critical content by default in generated landing pages.
- Any condition must be explainable in the section plan and compatible with the target site.
- If the condition depends on plugin-provided data, require live verification before relying on it.
